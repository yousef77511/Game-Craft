Development Phases

Ideation & Planning

Brainstorm game concepts, create designs, and pitch your ideas. Research game mechanics and art styles.

Prototyping

Build a functional prototype focusing on core mechanics without worrying about polish.

Development Sprint

Full coding, art, and audio creation. Daily check-ins and mid-week debugging session.

Polish & Playtesting

Refine your game, add polish, fix bugs, and get feedback from peers.

Submission

Submit your game with a demo video, screenshots, and description.

Showcase & Awards

Present your game in a live showcase and compete for fun awards.
